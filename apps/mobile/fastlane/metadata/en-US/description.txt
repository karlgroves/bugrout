BugRout is a navigation app designed for emergency evacuations. Unlike standard navigation that optimizes for speed under normal conditions, BugRout optimizes for safe arrival during infrastructure stress events.

KEY FEATURES

• Offline Navigation — Download regional maps and navigate with zero cell service. Routing, fuel stations, water sources, and threat zones all work offline.

• Predictive Congestion Routing — Our Evacuation Load Factor (ELF) model avoids roads that historically clog during mass evacuations, routing you through viable alternatives others won't think of.

• Threat-Aware Routing — Active wildfire perimeters, flood zones, and severe weather polygons are overlaid on the map and automatically avoided during route calculation.

• Resource Finder — Locate fuel stations, water sources, and emergency shelters along your route. Add resource stops with a single tap.

• 3-Tap Activation — Launch → Bug Out → Go. Pre-configure up to 3 evacuation scenarios with destinations, avoidance zones, and resource preferences for instant activation.

• Emergency Contacts — One-tap SMS sends your live location, destination, and ETA to up to 5 emergency contacts.

• Privacy First — No account required. Location processed on-device only. Optional anonymous crowd signal uses rotating tokens with 48-hour retention. No advertising. No tracking.

DATA SOURCES
Maps from OpenStreetMap. Weather alerts from the National Weather Service. Fire perimeters from USFS/NIFC. Flood zones from FEMA. Fuel stations from NREL. Water sources from USGS.

IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER
BugRout provides advisory routing only. Route suggestions are based on statistical models and may not reflect real-time conditions. Do not rely solely on this app for life-safety decisions. Always follow official evacuation orders.
